* Docs
** Keybindings
*** *C-[h,j,k,l]* move focus
*** *C-S-[h,j,k,l]* move focused window
*** "C-<down/up>" Move line
** Insert keybindings
*** *C-b* Move one word backward
*** *C-e* Move one word forward
*** *C-p* Yank from internal clipboard
*** *C-S-v* Paste from system clipboard
** Visual Keybindings
*** *C-S-c* Copy to system clipboard
